Lucid EV Motors

Dream Ahead

Lucid Motors is an American electric vehicle manufacturer founded in 2007 and headquartered in Newark, California. Originally known as Atieva, the company first focused on electric powertrains and battery technology before shifting its efforts to creating high-performance electric cars. Lucid aims to redefine luxury EVs by offering cutting-edge technology, exceptional design, and industry-leading driving range. The company’s first production model, the Lucid Air, is a luxury electric sedan that has received global attention for its impressive specifications. It offers a sleek design, a spacious interior, and an electric range that surpasses many competitors. The Air showcases Lucid’s advanced battery technology, which was developed through years of expertise in electric power systems. Lucid manufactures its vehicles at its Advanced Manufacturing Plant in Casa Grande, Arizona. This facility was built specifically for electric vehicle production and represents Lucid’s commitment to American manufacturing. The company has invested heavily in scaling up production to meet growing demand while maintaining premium quality standards. In addition to the Air, Lucid is developing new models to expand its lineup. The upcoming Lucid Gravity, a luxury electric SUV, is designed to compete in the popular SUV market while offering the same performance, efficiency, and luxury as the Air. Lucid also plans to introduce more affordable models in the future to reach a wider customer base. Backed by significant investment from Saudi Arabia’s Public Investment Fund, Lucid is positioning itself as a strong competitor to established luxury automakers. By combining advanced engineering, sustainable technology, and sophisticated design, Lucid Motors aims to play a major role in shaping the future of electric mobility worldwide.
