Jetour is a Chinese automobile brand created under the Chery Holding Group. It was introduced in 2018 to provide stylish and affordable SUVs for families and travelers. The name Jetour combines “Jet” and “Tour,” symbolizing speed, adventure, and comfortable travel. The brand focuses on making vehicles that are practical for daily use but also suitable for long journeys. The company’s lineup includes popular models such as the Jetour X70, X90, and Dashing series. These SUVs are known for spacious interiors, modern infotainment systems, and strong safety features. Jetour has also begun offering plug-in hybrids and electric options, like the eVT5, to meet the growing demand for cleaner transportation. Jetour has expanded beyond China into global markets, including Africa, the Middle East, and Southeast Asia. In countries like Nigeria, Jetour is gaining attention for providing well-equipped vehicles at competitive prices. Its strategy includes building a wide sales and service network to make ownership easier for customers in different regions. The brand invests heavily in research and development to improve performance, efficiency, and smart technology in its vehicles. Jetour aims to deliver not only affordable pricing but also advanced features such as driver-assistance systems and high-quality interiors. By blending technology and value, it positions itself as a strong competitor in the SUV market. Looking to the future, Jetour plans to enter the European market and expand its range of electric vehicles. Its goal is to become a major global player by offering dependable, eco-friendly, and innovative cars. If successful, Jetour could continue to grow and challenge more established automakers around the world.
