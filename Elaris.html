Elaris EV Motors

Revolutionizing mobility with solar-charged efficiency

Elaris is an example of a trend in the EV market: non-legacy companies using global supply chains (especially Chinese EV tech) to bring more affordable EVs to markets like Europe. If successful, such models can accelerate EV adoption by lowering entry barriers. But the insolvency suggests that scaling and sustaining such operations (with customer expectations and cost structures in Europe) is not trivial.
