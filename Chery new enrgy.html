Chery EV Motors

The leader of green, smart travel.

Chery New Energy is a division of Chery Automobile, a Chinese car manufacturer focused on developing electric and hybrid vehicles. It was created to support China’s push toward cleaner transportation and to compete in the rapidly growing electric vehicle market. The company designs and produces a range of electric cars, including compact city vehicles and small SUVs. These models are aimed at providing affordable, eco-friendly options for everyday drivers. By offering budget-friendly prices, Chery New Energy makes electric mobility more accessible to a wider audience. Chery New Energy emphasizes research and development to improve battery technology, driving range, and charging speed. It also works on smart features like advanced driver assistance systems and connected car technology to stay competitive in the EV market. Sustainability is a core value for Chery New Energy. The company uses energy-efficient manufacturing processes and focuses on reducing vehicle emissions. Its efforts support global goals for cleaner air and reduced carbon footprints. Today, Chery New Energy is expanding its presence in both domestic and international markets. Through innovation and affordable electric cars, it plays an important role in shaping the future of sustainable transportation.
