Quick overview

The Koenigsegg Regera RS is a high-performance evolution of the Regera, blending monstrous hybrid power with luxury, technology and record-breaking acceleration. Designed to eliminate traditional gearing, it delivers instant torque and extreme efficiency.

History — how it came to be

The standard Koenigsegg Regera debuted in 2015 as a revolutionary hybrid megacar featuring the Koenigsegg Direct Drive (KDD) system. Its mission was to replace traditional multi-gear transmissions with a single-speed solution, using electric motors to fill torque gaps instantly.

The Regera RS concept builds on that legacy, incorporating lighter materials, refined cooling, updated software and even more aggressive aerodynamics. Inspired by the success of Koenigsegg’s “RS” models such as the Agera RS, the Regera RS aims to push acceleration, handling and high-speed stability to new heights.

Key specifications

  • Engine: 5.0L twin-turbo V8 hybrid
  • Electric power: Three motors + battery assist
  • Total output: ≈1500–1600 hp (combined)
  • Top speed: ~410 km/h (electronically influenced)
  • 0–100 km/h: ~2.8 seconds
  • 0–400–0 record: Regera platform known for world-leading times
  • Drive: Rear-wheel drive with hybrid torque fill
  • Price: Multi-million dollar hypercar

Why it matters

The Regera RS represents Koenigsegg’s pursuit of hybrid innovation without compromise. It combines insane performance with elegant engineering, proving that hybrid systems can enhance—not replace—raw combustion power.
