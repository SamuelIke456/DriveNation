We Move Dreams

**Pininfarina** is an Italian car design and manufacturing company founded in 1930 by **Battista “Pinin” Farina**. Headquartered in Cambiano, Italy, the brand is famous for its elegant and timeless automotive designs. Over the decades, Pininfarina has worked with legendary carmakers such as **Ferrari, Maserati, Alfa Romeo, and Peugeot**, creating some of the most iconic cars in automotive history. Its designs are known for their beauty, balance, and aerodynamic precision, making Pininfarina a symbol of Italian creativity and craftsmanship. In recent years, Pininfarina has evolved from being just a design studio into a full-fledged car manufacturer. The company launched its first electric hypercar, the **Battista**, named after its founder. The Battista is one of the fastest and most powerful electric cars in the world, combining stunning Italian design with cutting-edge technology. It can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in under two seconds and has a top speed of over 350 km/h, showcasing Pininfarina’s ability to blend art and engineering excellence. Beyond cars, Pininfarina also designs **yachts, architecture, and industrial products**, proving its versatility in multiple design fields. Its approach emphasizes sustainability, innovation, and aesthetics — values that have helped it stay relevant for nearly a century. The company’s design philosophy focuses on **purity, elegance, and emotion**, ensuring that every creation stands out as a masterpiece. Today, **Automobili Pininfarina**, the company’s car division, represents the future of luxury and sustainable performance. With vehicles like the Battista, it aims to lead the transition into high-end electric mobility while keeping true to its Italian design heritage. Pininfarina remains a global icon in both automotive design and innovation, inspiring the next generation of car enthusiasts and designers worldwide.
